## Working hours

Standard working hours are 09:00–18:00 with a one-hour break. Flexible start
times between 07:00 and 10:00 are permitted with manager approval.

## Remote work

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week. Equipment requests for
home offices are handled by the IT help desk.

## Paid leave

Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid leave per year. Leave requests should
be submitted at least three business days in advance.

## On-call

Production on-call rotates weekly. The on-call hotline and escalation path are
maintained in the operations runbook.
