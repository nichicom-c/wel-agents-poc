# Internal FAQ

Synthetic sample document for the RAG PoC. Not real WEL-MOTHER content.

## How do I request access to a new system?

Open a ticket in the internal service desk with your manager's approval. Access
is granted within two business days.

## What is the expense reimbursement deadline?

Submit expense reports within 30 days of purchase. Reports submitted after the
deadline require a written explanation.

## Who do I contact for IT support?

Contact the IT help desk via the #it-support channel. For urgent incidents
outside business hours, use the on-call hotline listed in the handbook.
